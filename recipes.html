Chocolate

Lay the crepe onto a serving plate and warm for a few seconds in the microwave.Drizzle the warmed crepe with chocolate sauce.
Finish with whipped cream and scatter with chocolate shavings.

Vanilla

Warm the brownie in the microwave or oven as instructed. Scoop generously and place the ice cream scoops on the warm brownie.
Finish with a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

Raspberry

Warm the muffin and cut it into 3 parts.
Place the Raspberry sorbet scoop on top of the muffin, and Vanilla scoop between the muffin parts.
Finish with raspberry sauce and a sprinkle of almond nibs.
